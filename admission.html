Admission Overview

Feni Polytechnic Institute offers 4-year Diploma in Engineering courses under the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB). Admission is based on SSC results and available seats in each department.

Need Help?

  • admission@feni-poly.edu.bd

Eligibility

  • Minimum GPA 3.50 in SSC or equivalent examination
  • Minimum GPA 3.00 in Mathematics or Higher Mathematics
  • Must be a Bangladeshi citizen
  • Passed SSC in 2021, 2022, or 2023

Required Documents

  • Original SSC Marksheet and Certificate
  • Testimonial from Head of Institution
  • 4 copies of recent passport size photograph
  • Birth Registration Certificate
  • Parents' NID copy

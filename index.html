Feni Polytechnic Institute

Empowering Future Engineers Since 1963. We provide world-class
technical education to build the leaders of tomorrow.

Excellence in Technical Education

Feni Polytechnic Institute is one of the oldest and most prestigious
polytechnic institutes in Bangladesh. Located in the heart of Feni, we
have been shaping the future of engineering education for over six
decades.

Our campus features state-of-the-art laboratories, a rich library, modern
classrooms, and a vibrant community of students and faculty dedicated
to innovation and excellence.

Our Departments

We offer diploma engineering courses in five major technologies, each equipped
with modern facilities and expert faculty.

Computer

Software, networking, and modern computing systems.

Civil

Structural design, constrution, and surveying.

Electrical

Power systems, circuits and renewable enerfy.

Mechanical

Manufacturing thermodynamics, and machine design.

Electronics

Digital systems, microprocessors, and communication.
